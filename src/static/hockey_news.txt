# NHL Hockey News (May 2025)

Source: ESPN (https://www.espn.com/nhl/story/_/id/44698221/nhl-playoffs-2025-stanley-cup-bracket-schedule-teams-players-preview)

**Stanley Cup Playoffs Update**
*   **Summary:** The 2025 Stanley Cup playoffs are underway. The Winnipeg Jets are the top seed overall, and the Washington Capitals are the No. 1 seed in the East. Second-round matchups include Maple Leafs vs. Panthers, Capitals vs. Hurricanes, Jets vs. Stars, and Golden Knights vs. Oilers. Key players to watch include William Nylander (Maple Leafs), Sam Bennett (Panthers), Dylan Strome (Capitals), Sebastian Aho (Hurricanes), Mikko Rantanen (Stars), Kyle Connor (Jets), Jack Eichel (Golden Knights), and Connor McDavid (Oilers).
*   **Link:** https://www.espn.com/nhl/story/_/id/44698221/nhl-playoffs-2025-stanley-cup-bracket-schedule-teams-players-preview

**NHL Draft Lottery**
*   **Summary:** The New York Islanders won the 2025 NHL Draft Lottery, securing the No. 1 overall pick. The San Jose Sharks will pick second. Utah's new NHL team moved up to the 4th pick in the lottery.
*   **Link:** https://www.nbcsports.com/nhl/news/new-york-islanders-win-the-nhl-draft-lottery-securing-the-no-1-selection-sharks-will-pick-second
