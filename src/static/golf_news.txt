# Golf News (May 2025)

Source: PGA TOUR (https://www.pgatour.com/article/news/the-first-look/2025/05/05/preview-storylines-trusit-championship-the-philadelphia-cricket-club-rory-mcilroy-fedexcup-signature-event-field-aon) and other sources.

**Truist Championship (May 8-11, The Philadelphia Cricket Club)**
*   **Preview:** This is the sixth Signature Event on the PGA TOUR schedule. Rory McIlroy, a four-time winner of this event (formerly Wells Fargo Championship), is the defending champion and returns after his Masters win. Other top players competing include Xander Schauffele, Justin Thomas, and Collin Morikawa. World No. 1 Scottie Scheffler will not be playing.
*   **Field Notes:** Jordan Spieth, Rickie Fowler, Gary Woodland, and Keith Mitchell are among the sponsor exemptions.
*   **Course:** The Philadelphia Cricket Club (Wissahickon Course), a par 70, 7,119-yard A.W. Tillinghast design, restored by Keith Foster in 2013. It features 118 bunkers.
*   **Link:** https://www.pgatour.com/article/news/the-first-look/2025/05/05/preview-storylines-trusit-championship-the-philadelphia-cricket-club-rory-mcilroy-fedexcup-signature-event-field-aon

**PGA Championship (May 15-18, Quail Hollow Club, Charlotte, NC)**
*   **Preview:** The second men's major of the year. Scottie Scheffler is a key player to watch.
*   **Link:** https://www.espn.com/golf/schedule (for general schedule)

**Scottie Scheffler Wins The CJ Cup Byron Nelson**
*   **Summary:** Scottie Scheffler dominated The CJ Cup Byron Nelson, finishing at 31-under par for a historic win. This was his first win of 2025 and moved him to No. 2 in the Comcast Business Tour Top 10.
*   **Link:** https://sports.yahoo.com/golf/breaking-news/article/scottie-scheffler-runs-away-with-the-cj-cup-byron-nelson-matches-pga-tour-scoring-record-in-historic-win-221204044.html
