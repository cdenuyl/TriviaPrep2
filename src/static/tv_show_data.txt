# Top TV Shows (May 2025)

Source: IndieWire (https://www.indiewire.com/gallery/new-tv-shows-may-2025-preview/)

**1. The Four Seasons**
*   **Platform:** Netflix
*   **Release Date:** May 1, 2025
*   **Summary:** Based on Alan Alda’s 1981 film, this series follows three couples whose dynamic is threatened when they learn that two of them might break up. Over the course of a year they take four vacations while recovering from the jarring news.
*   **Stars:** Tina Fey, Will Forte, Steve Carell, Kerri Kenney-Silver, Colman Domingo, Marco Calvani

**2. Forever**
*   **Platform:** Netflix
*   **Release Date:** May 8, 2025
*   **Summary:** Adapted from Judy Blume’s 1975 novel, this series updates the setting to 2018 Los Angeles and follows two Black elementary school classmates who reconnect as high school juniors. It explores their relationship and the complexities of teen emotions in the internet age.
*   **Stars:** Michael Cooper Jr., Lovie Simone

**3. Duster**
*   **Platform:** Max
*   **Release Date:** May 15, 2025
*   **Summary:** From J.J. Abrams and LaToya Morgan, this series follows a getaway driver working for a crime family in the 1970s. With the family under scrutiny from federal agents, he must decide where his loyalties lie.
*   **Stars:** Josh Holloway, Rachel Hilson, Keith David, Sydney Elisabeth, Greg Grunberg

**4. Overcompensating**
*   **Platform:** Prime Video
*   **Release Date:** May 15, 2025
*   **Summary:** A closeted high school valedictorian arrives at college at a crossroads. He finds a kindred spirit, and together they explore college’s myriad opportunities and embarrassments.
*   **Stars:** Benito Skinner, Wally Baram, Mary Beth Barone, Adam DiMarco, Rish Shah, Connie Britton, Kyle MacLachlan

**5. Murderbot**
*   **Platform:** Apple TV+
*   **Release Date:** May 16, 2025
*   **Summary:** An adaptation of Martha Wells’ “The Murderbot Diaries,” this sci-fi series is about a security robot that overrides its protocols while continuing to work among humans, hiding its sentience.
*   **Stars:** Alexander Skarsgård, Noma Dumezweni, David Dastmalchian, Sabrina Wu, Akshay Khanna

**6. Motorheads**
*   **Platform:** Prime Video
*   **Release Date:** May 20, 2025
*   **Summary:** Two Brooklyn teens move to their mom’s hometown in Pennsylvania. Living with their uncle, they navigate a new town, a new high school, and questions about their missing father, all set against a backdrop of old cars and tense races.
*   **Stars:** Melissa Collazo, Michael Cimino, Nathalie Kelley, Ryan Phillippe, Mia Healey

**7. Sirens**
*   **Platform:** Netflix
*   **Release Date:** May 22, 2025
*   **Summary:** A darkly comic limited series about a wealthy socialite whose hold on her young protégé proves too much for her protective sister. It explores themes of modern role models, class disparity, and the influence of the wealthy.
*   **Stars:** Julianne Moore, Meghann Fahy, Milly Alcock, Kevin Bacon
